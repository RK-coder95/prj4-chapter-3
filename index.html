Spaceiguanas/my site

A prerecorded radio show where 2 youngsters’ question, discuss and explore music while riding a cloud of sounds. In simple words, 2 island boys chilling. Born and raised in the Caribbean, and ironically having the same name. The two Rolands crossed path a thousand miles away from home. And together they form a way to cope with change and familiarize themself with their reality. Music works as the mediator while they talk about the difference in perspectives encountered in life. Discussing challenges, accomplishments, the world, and their Caribbean roots. Each week Roland brings a compilation of sounds to explore together with the listeners. That’s two Rolands.
